# AutoRepHero — Content & Posting Strategy

## 🎯 Platforms (Priority Order)

### 1. YouTube (TOP PRIORITY)
**Why:** #1 platform for product demos, tutorials, and trust-building. Videos rank in Google Search. Evergreen content that sells for you 24/7.

**Content types:**
- Product demos ("How AutoRepHero works in 2 minutes")
- Problem/solution ("Why your Google listing could get suspended")
- How-to tutorials ("How to get more Google reviews without asking twice")
- RRDS Kit unboxing ("What's inside the Rapid Review Deployment Kit")
- NFC card demo ("Watch a customer leave a review in 30 seconds")
- Client case studies ("How [Business] went from 8 to 40 reviews in 60 days")
- Local SEO tips ("3 things killing your Google ranking right now")
- Review response examples ("How to respond to a 1-star review")
- AI review starters walkthrough
- Google TOS explainers ("What Google will suspend you for")

**Posting cadence:** 1-2 videos/week
**Video length:** 2-5 minutes for demos, 5-10 for tutorials
**Shorts:** Clip key moments into 60-second YouTube Shorts

---

### 2. LinkedIn
**Why:** B2B credibility. Local business owners, realtors, contractors — they're all on LinkedIn. This is where you build authority.

**Content types:**
- Share YouTube videos with commentary
- Quick tips about local SEO / reputation management
- Results posts ("Just helped a plumber go from 8 to 40 reviews in 60 days")
- Industry insights ("92% of consumers read reviews before choosing a local business")
- Behind-the-scenes ("Building out the RAPID system this week")
- Engage on local business groups
- Connect with Spokane business owners directly

**Posting cadence:** 3-5 posts/week
**Format:** Text posts with an image or video link. Keep it conversational.

---

### 3. NOT Priority Right Now
- **Facebook** — wrong audience for B2B SaaS. Revisit when you have a community/group.
- **Instagram** — visual platform, not where biz owners discover tools. Maybe later for short reels.
- **TikTok** — potential for viral short clips but not where your buyers hang out yet.
