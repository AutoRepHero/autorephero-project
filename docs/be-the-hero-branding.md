## 🎨 BRANDING IDENTITY — Current vs. Proposed

### Current Logo Analysis (from the image):
- Shield icon with speech bubble + sparkles — strong, says "protection + communication"
- Color gradient: teal/cyan → deep blue
- "Auto" in grey/silver, "Rep" in teal, "Hero" in dark blue
- Clean, professional, modern

### Proposed Enhancements:

**Keep:**
- ✅ The shield — it says "protection" which is perfect for reputation
- ✅ The speech bubble inside — reviews = communication
- ✅ The sparkles — quality, AI, premium
- ✅ The blue/teal gradient — trust, technology, growth
- ✅ "Hero" in bold blue — it's the power word

**Enhance:**
- Make "Hero" more prominent — it's the emotional word, should pop more
- Consider making "Auto" smaller or lighter — "RepHero" is the memorable part
- Add "Be the Hero." as a permanent tagline under the logo
- The shield could double as a badge/seal of trust

## 🎯 BRAND VOICE GUIDELINES

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Be bold and confident | Be arrogant or pushy |
| Use "hero" naturally, not forced | Say "hero" in every sentence |
| Speak to the business owner as a peer | Talk down to them |
| Use action words (deploy, launch, build) | Use passive language |
| Keep it short and punchy | Write paragraphs when bullets work |
| Show results with numbers | Make vague promises |
| Reference the ⚡ bolt naturally | Overuse emojis |

### Brand Personality:
- **Confident** but not cocky
- **Action-oriented** — deploy, launch, build, grow
- **Protective** — shield, guard, defend your reputation
- **Empowering** — YOU are the hero, we're the tools
- **Local-first** — we understand small business
