📱 How to Use Your Kit

  • Hand the NFC card to happy customers
  • They tap it on the back of their phone
  • Your Review Hub opens — they pick a platform
  • AI helps them write a personalized review
  • Keep the table tent visible on your counter
  • Share your review link via text anytime

💬 What to Say

"I'm glad you're happy with the service! If you have a minute, we'd really appreciate a review — just tap this card or scan the code. It only takes 30 seconds and really helps us out."
"That means a lot, thank you! Would you mind sharing that as a review? It helps other people find us."

⚠️ Important — Google Rules

  • Never offer discounts or gifts for reviews
  • Never tell them what to write or what rating
  • Never write reviews for customers
  • Never ask only happy customers
Google can suspend your listing if you violate review policies. The AI starters are just ideas — customers MUST personalize with their own experience.

🤖 AI Review Starters

  • The app suggests review ideas to help customers start
  • Customers MUST add their own details
  • Tell them: "Add what service you got and what you liked"
  • Generic/copied reviews get flagged by Google
  • Personal reviews convert better anyway!
